Morphology of Flowering Plants

Covers the external structure of angiosperms — root, stem, leaf, inflorescence, flower, fruit and seed — plus floral diagrams, floral formulae and the diagnostic features of the Fabaceae, Solanaceae and Liliaceae families that NEET tests almost every year.
