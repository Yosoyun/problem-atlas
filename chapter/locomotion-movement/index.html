Locomotion and Movement

A high-yield Class XI Human Physiology chapter where students master muscle ultrastructure, the sliding filament mechanism, the human skeleton, joints, and skeletal-muscular disorders for NEET. Questions are tightly NCERT-statement based, so line-by-line recall of bands, bone counts, joint types and disorders pays off.
