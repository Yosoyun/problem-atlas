A high-yield Physical Chemistry chapter where JEE Advanced reliably tests integrated rate laws, half-life manipulation, and Arrhenius/activation-energy numericals. Train students to read order off initial-rate and graphical data and to interconvert tabular, graphical, and equation forms fluently.
