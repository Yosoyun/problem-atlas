Kinetic Theory of Gases

A high-yield bridge between mechanics and thermodynamics: drill students on the molecular model of gases — pressure from collisions, rms vs average vs most-probable speeds, degrees of freedom, equipartition, and mean free path — since these results feed directly into thermodynamics and calorimetry problems.
